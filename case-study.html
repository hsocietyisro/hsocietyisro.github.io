Why Hyderabad?

One of the major reason for choosing Hyderabad as a case study was that it is listed in world's top 10 fastest growing cities.
Hyderabad is the capital of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana states both.
As we can see in the above time-lapse video of change over the past 32 years. In fact, on the slight left side, fast changes are visible. How from 1984 when there was only Forrest and plateau, the main city expanded in all directions really fast.
